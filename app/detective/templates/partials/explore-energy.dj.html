About the project

This comprehensive knowledge base draws up an inventory of innovative energy projects in developing countries. It rigorously lists projects, their owners and key persons and the relationships that link them. Journalists, investors and development professionals now have access to a wide range of sourced and structured data. It will enable them to make better informed decision in a field that dramatically lacks scrutiny.

The project was funded by a grant by the European Journalism Center in cooperation with the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
